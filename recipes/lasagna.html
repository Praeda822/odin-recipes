Homemade Pasta Lasagna

Looking for a delicious and healthy Lasagna recipe you can make at home?

This spin on the classic Italian favorite gets the job done when it comes to satiating your appetite

2 hours
Serves 6

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 4 zucchini, cut into 1/4" rounds
  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced pancetta
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 2 cans (28oz/ea) San Marzano tomatoes, crushed by hand...
  • ..mixed with 1 cup water and sloshed around in one can
  • 3 dried bay leaves
  • 4 Tbsp butter, plus extra for greasing
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan
  • 2 cups shredded low-moisture mozzarella
  • 1 box (12 oz) lasagna noodles

Steps

    Build Your Bolognese

  1. Heat 3Tbsp of olive oil on medium-high in a large pot. Brown your garlic and your zucchini for about 2 minutes, then move it to a plate. Now brown your chicken for about 5-7 minutes, adding it to the plate with your garlic and zucchini.
  2. Next, saute your onions, pancetta, and add a pinch of of salt for every 1Tbsp of olive oil until the onion softens and the fat gets in (takes roughly 5 or so minutes). Now add the tomatoes, water, bay leaves, salt and papper, simmering until thickened. This should take about 20-30 minutes. Finally, add your plate ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes.

    3. Concoct the Secret Sauce

  3. Melt your butter in a medium-sized pan over medium heat, gradually adding the flour whilst whisking continuously over the heat until the mixtures appears glossy, which should take about 5 minutes.
  4. Now add your milk, whisking until the sauce mixture has thickened but is still pourable (takes about 5 minutes). Then season the sauce with salt, pouring it into a bowl and wrapping it with plastic wrap, ensuyring the plastic wrap touches the surface of the sauce (so there's no skin).

    5. Prepare your Pasta

  5. Preheat your oven to 180°C (375°F for the Americans) and butter up an 8"x 8" baking dish. Then mix the cheeses in one bowl and sauces into another. Add 1/2 cup of sauce to the dish, then a pasta layer, then 3Tbsp cheese, then some more pasta, layering the lasagna like a cake as you go.
  6. Layer sauce/cheese/pasta four times and don't be stingy with your sauce! Fold any leftover pasta over the lasagna and add any remaining sauce/cheese.

    7. The MasterChef Special

  7. Put aluminum foil over the dish ensuring you poke some holes in the foil so it can vent steam. Now set your lasagna on a baking sheet and bake until the top begins to bubble, which should take about 30-45 minutes.
  8. Finally, remove the foil cover and continue baking until the top of your lasagna is brown and crispy which will take another 30 minutes or so. Let your dish stand for another half hour before getting stuck into it!
